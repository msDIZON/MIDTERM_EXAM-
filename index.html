Calle Crisologo

Calle Crisologo is a famous cobblestone street located in Vigan City, Ilocos Sur, known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial houses. Walking along the street feels like stepping back in time, with antique shops, caf√©s, and museums lining both sides. At night, the street becomes even more charming with warm lights highlighting the historic architecture. Wind Mill

The windmills of Ilocos Norte, particularly in Bangui, are giant structures lined up along the shoreline facing the West Philippine Sea. They were built to harness wind energy, making them the first power-generating windmills in Southeast Asia. The sight of the tall white turbines against the sea and mountains has also become a popular tourist attraction. Aside from providing renewable energy, the windmills symbolize innovation and sustainability in the province.

Patapat Bridge

Patapat Bridge in Ilocos Norte is a scenic viaduct that stretches along the winding mountainsides near Pagudpud. It connects the Maharlika Highway to Cagayan Valley, making travel more convenient for motorists. The bridge offers breathtaking views of the sea on one side and lush mountains on the other. It is not only an important road link but also a favorite stop for tourists who want to enjoy its panoramic scenery.
